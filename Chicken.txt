1) Grilled Lemon Garlic Chicken
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tbsp lemon juice
3 minced garlic cloves
1 tsp dried oregano
salt and pepper

Instructions:
In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken in the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Marinate for at least 30 minutes or up to 24 hours in the fridge. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, until fully cooked.

2) Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 can corn (drained)
1 jar of salsa
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp smoked paprika

Instructions:
Add chicken, black beans, corn, salsa, chili powder, cumin, and smoked paprika to the slow cooker. Stir until the chicken is coated. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours. Shred chicken with a fork and serve in warm tortillas with desired toppings.

3) Baked Garlic Parmesan Chicken
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika
salt and pepper
2 beaten eggs
1 cup panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 tbsp butter (melted)
4 minced garlic cloves

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Mix flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a shallow bowl. In another bowl, whisk eggs. In a third bowl, mix panko breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese. Dredge chicken in flour mixture, shaking off excess. Dip chicken in eggs and coat with panko mixture. Place chicken in a greased baking dish. Mix melted butter and garlic and pour over chicken. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
