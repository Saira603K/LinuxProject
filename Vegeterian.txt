1)Creamy Vegan Mushroom Risotto
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup arborio rice
4 cups vegetable broth
1 cup mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup vegan cream cheese
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
salt and pepper

Instructions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add rice and stir to coat. Add 1 cup of broth and cook, stirring occasionally, until broth is absorbed. Repeat with remaining broth, adding 1 cup at a time and stirring occasionally, until rice is tender and creamy. Add mushrooms and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in vegan cream cheese and nutritional yeast until creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

2)Lentil Vegetable Soup
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
4 cups vegetable broth
1 cup lentils, rinsed
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried oregano
salt and pepper

Instructions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add broth, lentils, carrots, celery, tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 30 minutes, or until lentils and vegetables are tender.

3)Vegan Spinach and Mushroom Lasagna
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
10 oz spinach, chopped
1 jar tomato sauce
1/2 cup vegan ricotta cheese
12 oz lasagna noodles
1/2 cup vegan mozzarella cheese

Instructions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and spinach and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Spread 1 cup of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with a layer of noodles, then a layer of sauce, and a layer of ricotta cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce. Top with mozzarella cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
