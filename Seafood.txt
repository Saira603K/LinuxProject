1)Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi
Ingredients:
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup white wine
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
salt and pepper
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
8 oz spaghetti

Instructions:
Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes until pink. Add white wine and lemon juice, let it simmer for 1 minute. Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve shrimp scampi over cooked spaghetti and sprinkle chopped parsley on top.

2)Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill
Ingredients:
4 salmon fillets
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
2 garlic cloves, minced
salt and pepper

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°F. In a bowl, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, dill, and garlic. Place salmon fillets in a baking dish and pour the mixture over them. Season with salt and pepper. Bake for 12-15 minutes, until salmon is cooked through.

3)Spicy Shrimp Tacos
Ingredients:
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
salt and pepper
8 small corn tortillas
1 cup shredded cabbage
1 avocado, sliced
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions:
In a bowl, mix together shrimp, olive oil, cumin, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add shrimp. Cook for 2-3 minutes until pink. Warm the tortillas. Assemble the tacos by placing the shrimp on each tortilla and topping with cabbage, avocado, and cilantro. Squeeze lime juice over the tacos and serve.
